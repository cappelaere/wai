KEY CHARACTERISTICS
===================

Contrasting Examples (Good for showing range):
- Personal Agent: Strong aviator vs. Career-changer
- Academic Agent: High performer vs. Non-traditional path
- Recommendation Agent: Consensus endorsement vs. Mixed with concerns
- Social Agent: Strong presence vs. No presence
- Application Agent: Complete vs. Incomplete

Paired Examples (Show before/after or ideal/realistic):
- All agents have Example 1 showing strong/ideal case
- All agents have Example 2 showing realistic/challenging case

Common Themes:
- Aviation focus and commitment emphasized
- Different paths to success (not just perfect credentials)
- Clear scoring with detailed breakdowns
- Balanced perspective on candidates' strengths and growth areas
