# Academic Profile Evaluation Criteria

This file contains additional criteria that should be considered when evaluating academic profiles for this scholarship.

## Evaluation Focus Areas

1. **Academic Performance**
   - Prioritize applicants with strong academic records (high GPA, honors, etc.)
   - Consider consistency of academic performance over time
   - Value academic excellence in relevant subjects (STEM, aviation-related courses)
   - Assess academic standing and class rank if available

2. **Academic Relevance**
   - Prioritize applicants pursuing aviation-related programs or degrees
   - Value coursework relevant to aviation careers (engineering, physics, mathematics, etc.)
   - Consider alignment between academic program and scholarship goals
   - Assess relevance of academic background to aviation industry needs

3. **Academic Readiness**
   - Evaluate preparedness for aviation training or advanced studies
   - Consider academic foundation for success in aviation careers
   - Assess ability to handle rigorous academic and training programs
   - Value evidence of academic growth and improvement

4. **Academic Awards and Achievements**
   - Recognize academic honors, scholarships, and awards
   - Value participation in academic competitions or programs
   - Consider membership in honor societies or academic organizations
   - Assess recognition for academic excellence

## Scoring Guidelines

- Academic performance scores should reflect both GPA and consistency
- Academic relevance should reward aviation-related or STEM-focused programs
- Academic readiness should consider both current performance and potential
- Overall academic profile should balance performance, relevance, and readiness

## Scholarship-Specific Considerations

### Program Preferences

**Highly Valued:**
- Aviation-related degree programs (Aviation Management, Aerospace Engineering, etc.)
- STEM programs with aviation applications
- Programs that directly support aviation career goals

**Also Valued:**
- Strong academic performance in any field
- Evidence of academic excellence and commitment
- Academic awards and recognition

### GPA Considerations

- Minimum GPA requirements (if applicable)
- Consideration of GPA trends (improving vs. declining)
- Context for GPA (difficulty of program, institution rigor)
- Recognition that GPA is one factor among many

### Academic Awards and Honors

- Dean's List, Honor Roll, or similar recognition
- Academic scholarships or awards
- Membership in honor societies (Phi Beta Kappa, etc.)
- Academic competition participation or awards

## Additional Notes

- Transcripts provide the most reliable academic information
- Resume education sections may contain incomplete information
- Consider the context of the applicant's academic journey
- Academic readiness for aviation training is particularly important for flight training scholarships
