# Social Presence Profile Evaluation Criteria

This file contains additional criteria that should be considered when evaluating social media presence for this scholarship.

## Evaluation Focus Areas

1. **Professional Social Media Presence**
   - Prioritize applicants with professional LinkedIn profiles
   - Value LinkedIn profiles that demonstrate aviation interest and career focus
   - Consider completeness and professionalism of LinkedIn profiles
   - Assess LinkedIn activity and engagement in aviation-related content

2. **Aviation Engagement on Social Media**
   - Look for evidence of aviation-related content or engagement
   - Value social media presence that demonstrates passion for aviation
   - Consider participation in aviation communities or groups
   - Assess sharing of aviation-related content, experiences, or achievements

3. **Professionalism and Appropriateness**
   - Evaluate overall professionalism of social media presence
   - Consider appropriateness of content for professional contexts
   - Value social media that reflects positively on the applicant
   - Assess alignment between social media presence and scholarship values

4. **Platform Diversity**
   - Consider presence across multiple professional platforms
   - Value LinkedIn as the primary professional platform
   - Consider other platforms (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok) for aviation engagement
   - Assess consistency of professional image across platforms

## Scoring Guidelines

- Professional presence scores should emphasize LinkedIn quality and completeness
- Aviation engagement scores should reflect demonstrated interest and participation
- Professionalism scores should consider appropriateness and alignment with values
- Overall social profile should balance professional presence and aviation engagement

## Scholarship-Specific Considerations

### Platform Priorities

**Primary Focus:**
- **LinkedIn** - Professional networking and career development
  - Complete profile with aviation-related experience
  - Recommendations and endorsements
  - Engagement with aviation industry content
  - Professional connections in aviation

**Secondary Consideration:**
- **Instagram** - Visual content and aviation engagement
  - Aviation-related photos or content
  - Engagement with aviation communities
  - Professional or educational content

- **Facebook** - Community engagement
  - Participation in aviation groups or pages
  - Professional or educational content
  - Community involvement

- **TikTok** - Content creation and engagement
  - Aviation-related content creation
  - Educational or informative content
  - Positive engagement with aviation community

### What to Look For

**Positive Indicators:**
- Professional LinkedIn profile with aviation focus
- Active engagement with aviation content or communities
- Sharing of aviation experiences, achievements, or learning
- Professional connections in the aviation industry
- Content that demonstrates passion and commitment to aviation

**Neutral Indicators:**
- Limited social media presence (not necessarily negative)
- Personal social media without aviation focus
- Private or minimal public profiles

**Potential Concerns:**
- Inappropriate or unprofessional content
- Content that conflicts with scholarship values
- Negative or concerning online behavior
- Lack of professional social media presence (may indicate limited networking)

## Additional Notes

- Social media presence is optional but can provide additional context
- Professional LinkedIn presence is particularly valuable for career-focused scholarships
- Aviation engagement on social media demonstrates passion and community involvement
- Absence of social media presence should not negatively impact evaluation
- Focus on professional and aviation-related content, not personal social media
